About DataLoom

This tool leverages a powerful backend to automatically analyze your CSV files. It intelligently detects data types, identifies missing values, and applies your chosen imputation strategy (or an intelligent 'auto' mode).

You'll receive a cleaned dataset, a JSON summary of all changes, and a human-readable text report, including a 'Data Health Score' to quantify the improvements.

DataLoom is designed for speed, accuracy, and ease of use, empowering analysts, students, and developers to spend less time cleaning data and more time deriving insights.
